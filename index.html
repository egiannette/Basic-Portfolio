About Me

I am brand new to coding and really anything other than the basics dealing with computers. I have worked as an Animal Protection Officer for the City of Austin specializing in tethering and neglect. I am currently working as a hospice liasion for a local company. My job has been very rewarding but I am ready for something different. I would like to branch out and master a new skill so that I may change careers in the future.

In my free time I enjoy volunteering at Hopeful Hearts Equine Rehabilitation and Paws Great Dane Rescue of Austin. I am also involved with PMAD, a group that is trying to end the use of puppy mills.
